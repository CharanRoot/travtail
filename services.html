Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

Travtail Services is an on-demand application for errands and other services that is not linked to specific stores/restaurants like Postmates and Instacart. You can basically run any errand requested by our customers.

How is this different from Postmates, DoorDash and Instacart?

Our mission is to help you achieve financial independence and not to complete as many deliveries as possible. 2. Unlike Posmates and/or Instacart where you have to complete multiple deliveries in the shortest possible time and still make less than $20/hr., you will get full payment for each errand you run. For example, if you make $100 running an errand for 1hr, that money is all yours! We only take a fixed 15% commission. No pressure to complete multiple jobs. Work whenever and however you like.

How much money can I make?

Average sellers can make $600-$1000 a month providing services. Top sellers can make $2000-$5000 a month providing services. If you offer high demand services, you can make up to $10K a month

What fees do you charge?

We charge a fixed 15% commission.

Which locations are you in?

We are currently only in Nashville. We will be expanding to more locations in the US in the next 12 months. You can sign up to offer services on Travtail in any location in the US - complete this form to sign up. For other questions, please email us at contact@travtail.com.

About Us

Travtail provides software and hardware that enables short-term rental owners, rideshare drivers, entrepreneurs, craftspersons, service providers, hotels, consumer brands, and small & large business to sell products and services to their customers.

Our Mission is to provide the most cost-effective, efficient and technology-enabled commerce tools for unmanned retail and on-demand services

Our Vision is to build the world’s largest community of micro-entrepreneurs who are able to make livable wages doing what they love and from any location in the world.
