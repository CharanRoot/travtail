Frequently Asked Questions

How does this work?

Travtail Services is an on-demand application for errands and other services that are not linked to specific stores/restaurants like Postmate/Instacart - there is no pressure to complete multiple jobs. Unlike Thumbtack, we don't do quotes or charge you to contact customers. Think the price offered by the customer is fair for the task? Be the first person to accept it! You can basically run any errand requested by our customers.

How is this different from Postmates, DoorDash and Instacart?

Our mission is to help you achieve financial independence. Unlike Posmates and/or Instacart where you have to complete multiple deliveries in the shortest possible time and still make less than $20/hr., you will get full payment for each errand you run. For example, if you make $100 running an errand for 1hr, that money is all yours! We only take a fixed 15% commission. No pressure to complete multiple errands or accept low-value ones. Work whenever and however you like.

How much money can I make?

Average "Travelers" can make $600-$1000 a month providing services. Top Travelers can make $2000-$5000 a month providing services. If you offer high demand services, you can make up to $10K a month

What fees do you charge?

We charge a fixed 15% commission.

Which locations are you in?

We are currently only in Nashville. We will be expanding to more locations in the US in the next 12 months. You can sign up to offer services on Travtail in any location in the US - complete this form to sign up. For other questions, please email us at contact@travtail.com.

About Us

Travtail builds unmanned retail and on-demand services solutions for the world’s on-demand economy. Our products enable customers to get essential products and services right now, not tomorrow or next week. Our vision is to be the world’s first end-to-end convenience services company.

We are building commerce tools (software, hardware) that will enable our clients (rental property owners, rideshare drivers, property/leasing managers, hotels/motels and micro-entrepreneurs) to sell essential products and enable their customers to find essential services in less than 30s.
