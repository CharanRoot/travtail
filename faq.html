Frequently Asked Questions

What is Travtail?

Travtail provides provide software and hardware that enables short-term rental owners, rideshare drivers, service providers, hotels, consumer brands, small and large business to sell products and services to 2B+ travelers.

How do I sign up?

Complete this form.

How will you know when I need new products?

We are constantly analyzing each store to identify potential stockouts, high turnover items and several other data points and we can predict with almost 100% accuracy when you’ll need new products and ensure they are delivered to you on time.

How will I receive products?

We will ship the products to your desired location.

How much money can I make?

You can make $200-$5000/month. Average sellers can make $200-$500 a month selling products and $600-$1000 a month providing services. Top sellers can make $600-$1000 a month selling products and $2000-$5000 a month providing services.

Do you require background checks for service providers?

Yes, all service providers must go through a quick background check online. The process is fast and easy.

Which locations are you in?

Currently, we are in San Francisco, Los Angeles, New York, Atlanta and Nashville. We will be expanding to more short term rentals and rideshares in these locations and across the US in the next 12 months. You can sign up to sell on Travtail in any location in the US, complete this form to sign up

How many stores can I have?

You can have as many stores as you need. If your short term rental property gets a lot of traffic, we recommend putting one box in each room. For rideshare drives, you can have up to two boxes in your car, one in the passenger section and one in the driver section.All completely self-service.
