Frequently Asked Questions

What is Travtail?

Travtail provides software and hardware that enables short-term rental owners, entrepreneurs, rideshare drivers, craftpersons, service providers, enterpreneurs, hotels, consumer brands, and small & large business to sell products and services to 2B+ travelers and other customers. We have stores that cater for different locations (Short-Term Rentals, Uber/Lyft/Taxi, Motels, Hotels, Apartment Complexes, Shopping Mall etc.) and provide our clients with all the tools they need to succeed.

How do I sign up?

Complete this form.

How will you know when I need new products?

We are constantly analyzing each store to identify potential stockouts, high turnover items and several other data points and can predict with almost 100% accuracy when you’ll need new products. If you are selling Travtail Products, we will ensure they are delivered to you on time. If you are buying your own products, we will give you access to several tools to plan for stockouts and manage your inventory. Our partners can give you discounts on wholesale purchases to reload your store - you have access to these discounts for free.

How will I receive products?

We will ship the products to your desired location (this only applies to select locations). If you are selling your own products, you can buy products to reload your store whenever you want but we will provide you with several tools to make sure you don't run out of stock.

How much money can I make?

You can make $200-$5000/month. Average sellers can make $200-$500 a month selling products and $600-$1000 a month providing services. Top sellers can make $600-$1000 a month selling products and $2000-$5000 a month providing services. If you sell high value products,buy the bigger stores and/or provide high demand services, you can make up to $10K a month

Do you require background checks for service providers?

Yes, all service providers must go through a quick background check online. The process is fast and easy.

Which locations are you in?

We will be in San Francisco, Los Angeles, New York, Atlanta and Nashville in the next few weeks. We will be expanding to more short term rentals and rideshares in these locations and across the US in the next 12 months. You can sign up to sell on Travtail in any location in the US - complete this form to sign up.

How many stores can I have?

You can have as many stores as you need. If your short term rental property, rideshare, apartment complex, hotel or any other location gets a lot of traffic, we recommend putting our stores in multiple locations (in different rooms, hallways, floors etc.) - we have stores of different sizes and shapes to accomodate the unique needs of specific locations. For rideshare drivers, you can have up to three stores in your car - one between the front passenger and driver seat, one behind the passenger seat and another behind the driver seat. All stores are completely self-service - put the store anywhere and continue with your normal activities.

What types of products can I sell?

In general, you can sell any product or offer any service (as long as you have the required experience or qualification). As part of our onboarding, we will give you access to our "Product and Service" ideas training - this training will give you insights into high demand products based on where, when and how you plan to sell your products or offer services. We also offer a different product, “Travtail Commerce”. With Travtail Commerce, you will be able to sell products in your store and to customers in any location in the world. Sign up here to be notified as soon as Travtail Commerce is available. To suggest a new product or service offering, send us a message via the contact us page or send an email to contact@travtail.com.

What types of services can I offer?

You can offer any service (as long as you have the required experience or qualification). If you will like to offer services to your guests or other guests nearby, please sign up using this form. Services that you can offer include: massage services, bag packing, travel concierge, travel agent, travel planner, personal assistant, cleaning services, personal shopper, personal helper, chauffeur, car rental, bike rental, hair stylist, barber, personal local guide, laundry, dry cleaning, dog walker, general delivery helper, luggage storage, chef, personal tours/events guide. Sign up here to start offering services! To suggest a new service that we are not currently offering, send us a message via the contact us page or contact us via contact@travtail.com.

About Us

Travtail builds unmanned retail and on-demand services solutions for the world’s on-demand economy. Our products enable customers to get essential products and services right now, not tomorrow or next week. Our vision is to be the world’s first end-to-end convenience services company.

We are building commerce tools (software, hardware) that will enable our clients (rental property owners, rideshare drivers, property/leasing managers, hotels/motels and micro-entrepreneurs) to sell essential products and enable their customers to find essential services in less than 30s.
